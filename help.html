1. Getting Started

Welcome to PropertyTracker! This guide will help you make the most of the app.

Quick Start

Tap the "Add Item" card on the home screen to start adding items to your inventory.

2. Adding Items

1

Tap Add Item

Tap the "Add Item" card on the main screen to open the add item form.

2

Enter Item Name

Type the item name (required). The app will suggest matching items from the database to help you enter names quickly.

3

Select Location

Choose a storage location from the preset list or enter a custom location.

4

Set Expiration (Optional)

Enable expiration tracking and set a date for items that have shelf life.

5

Add Notes

Add any additional notes or details about the item.

3. Adding Photos

You can add photos to items in two ways:

From Photo Library

Tap "Select from Album" to choose an existing photo from your device's photo library.

Take Photo

Tap "Take Photo" to open the camera and capture a new photo of the item.

Camera Focus

Tap anywhere on the camera preview to set the focus point. A yellow indicator will show the focused area.

4. Storage Locations

PropertyTracker includes preset locations to help you organize items:

  • Kitchen - Kitchen items and food
  • Living Room - Living room items
  • Bedroom - Bedroom items
  • Study - Books, documents, office supplies
  • Entryway - Keys, bags, everyday carry items
  • Refrigerator - Food and beverages
  • Bathroom - Toiletries, medicine
  • Balcony - Outdoor items, plants
  • Storage Room - Seasonal items, tools
  • Garage - Car accessories, tools

You can also enter custom locations like "Desk Drawer", "Bookshelf", etc.

5. Expiration Tracking

Enable expiration dates for items to get timely reminders:

Status Indicators

  • Normal - Sufficient time remaining
  • Expiring Soon (Orange) - Less than 7 days remaining
  • Expired (Red) - Past expiration date
Use Case Examples

Track expiration for food items, medicine, cosmetics, batteries, warranty documents, and seasonal items.

6. Editing & Deleting Items

1

Open Item

Tap on any item in the list to open the edit view.

2

Modify Details

Change the name, location, photo, expiration date, or notes as needed.

3

Save or Delete

Tap "Save" to keep your changes, or scroll down to "Delete Item" to remove it.

7. Searching Items

Use the search bar at the top of the home screen to find items quickly. You can search by:

  • Item name
  • Storage location
  • Notes content
Quick Tip

Search supports partial matching, so typing "眼" will find items with "蒸汽眼罩" in the name.

8. Exporting Data

PropertyTracker allows you to export all your item data:

1

Open Settings

Tap the gear icon in the top-left corner of the main screen.

2

Export Data

Tap "Export Item Data" in the Settings menu.

3

Choose Format

Select "Data Only" to export item information, or "Include Images" to also export photos.

4

Share

Choose how to share - save to Files, send via email, AirDrop, or any other app.

9. Settings

Dark Mode

Toggle dark mode to switch between light and dark appearance. Your preference is saved automatically.

User Manual

Access this help guide anytime from Settings > User Manual.

10. Tips & Tricks

Item Suggestions

When adding items, the app suggests names from a built-in database of 20,000+ common items and 10,000+ books. Just tap a suggestion to fill it in quickly.

Recent Locations

The location selector shows your recently used locations first, making it faster to add items to common storage spots.

Swipe to Delete

Swipe left on any item in the list to quickly delete it.

Grouped View

Items are automatically grouped by storage location, making it easy to see everything in one place.

11. FAQ

Q: Is my data backed up automatically?

A: No, data is stored only on your device. We recommend using the export feature regularly to create backups.

Q: Can I use the app on multiple devices?

A: Data is not synced between devices. You can export your data on one device and import on another (future feature).

Q: How do I remove a photo from an item?

A: Open the item, tap on the photo, then tap the X button to remove it.

Q: What happens when an item expires?

A: The app marks it as "Expired" in red. You can then edit or delete the item as needed.

Q: Can I change the language?

A: The app follows your device's language settings. Change your iOS language in Settings > General > Language & Region.

Data Loss Warning

Uninstalling the app will delete all your data. Always export your data regularly to avoid losing important information.
